Paneer Tikka Pizza

Home

Description

Sarbloh Bibek friendly Paneer Tikka Pizza with spiced paneer and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Pizza Base
  • Fresh Paneer cubes
  • Tomato puree (no onion/garlic)
  • Capsicum
  • Yogurt marinade
  • Black pepper
  • Rock salt
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Marinate paneer in yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook lightly on a pan.
  3. Spread sauce on base.
  4. Add toppings.
  5. Bake at 200°C for 12–15 minutes.
  6. Serve hot.
