Veggie Sarbloh Wrap

Home

Description

Simple Sarbloh Bibek wrap with fresh vegetables and paneer.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat chapati
  • Paneer slices
  • Capsicum
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Rock salt
  • Black pepper
  • Yogurt sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare chapati.
  2. Add fillings.
  3. Roll tightly.
  4. Serve fresh.
