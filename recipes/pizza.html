Pizza

Description

Hey! Today we are learning how to make a Sarbloh Bibek friendly pizza. This recipe is inspired by the Gurmat Bibek YouTube Channel.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Paneer (Sarbloh Bibeki prepared)
  • Homemade Pizza Sauce (no onion/garlic if following strict bibek)
  • Whole Wheat Pizza Base or Hand-rolled Dough
  • Fresh Tomatoes (finely sliced)
  • Green Capsicum (bell pepper)
  • Sweet Corn (optional)
  • Black Pepper
  • Rock Salt / Sendha Namak
  • Olive Oil or Mustard Oil (light drizzle)
  • Fresh Coriander (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough using whole wheat flour, water, salt, and a little oil. Knead well and let it rest for 1–2 hours.
  2. Roll out the dough into a pizza base of desired thickness.
  3. Prepare the pizza sauce (tomato-based, without onion or garlic if following strict Sarbloh Bibek).
  4. Spread a thin layer of sauce evenly over the base.
  5. Add sliced fresh paneer as the main topping.
  6. Add vegetables like capsicum, tomatoes, and corn evenly.
  7. Sprinkle rock salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Drizzle a small amount of oil over the toppings.
  9. Preheat the oven to 200°C and bake the pizza for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden.
  10. Remove from oven, garnish with fresh coriander, slice, and serve hot.
