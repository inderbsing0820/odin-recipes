Pancakes

Description

Healthy whole wheat pancakes made in Sarbloh Bibek style.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Milk or water
  • Banana
  • Ghee
  • Jaggery or salt

Steps

  1. Make batter.
  2. Heat pan with ghee.
  3. Cook pancakes both sides.
  4. Serve warm.
