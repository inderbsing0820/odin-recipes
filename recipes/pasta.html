Sarbloh Bibek Pasta

Description

Simple pasta made without onion or garlic in Sarbloh Bibek style.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat pasta
  • Tomato puree
  • Paneer cubes
  • Capsicum
  • Olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • Rock salt
  • Fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Prepare sauce.
  3. Add vegetables and paneer.
  4. Mix and cook briefly.
  5. Serve hot.
