Sarbloh Bibek Vegetable Rice

Description

Simple Bibek-friendly vegetable rice without onion or garlic.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Capsicum
  • Paneer cubes
  • Rock salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ghee or oil

Steps

  1. Cook rice.
  2. Sauté vegetables.
  3. Add paneer and spices.
  4. Mix with rice.
  5. Serve hot.
